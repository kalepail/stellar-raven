# Prior-art review - kalepail/stellar-raven PR #12

**Source:** `research/golden/_meta/_prior-art/_incoming/pr12-golden-questions.json` plus
`research/golden/_meta/_prior-art/_incoming/pr12.diff`  
**Scope:** Phase 1 primary review, PR #12 added questions only. Rubric blocks in the PR were ignored
except as wording hints.  
**Baseline:** `research/golden/_meta/_prior-art/_our-questions.txt` (391 current questions) and the
current golden schema/taxonomy as of 2026-06-23.

## Summary counts

- Extracted PR #12 added questions: **55**
- **COVERED:** 35
- **NEAR:** 14
- **NEW:** 6

Most additions are beginner/user-support variants of existing anchor, asset, wallet, path-payment,
DeFi, tokenomics, and validator questions. The residual value is concentrated in memo/memo-less
exchange deposits, consumer scam/recovery/security flows, merchant acceptance, node-ops setup depth,
and a few broad comparison/data-discovery shapes. Several are likely better as rejected or reframed
edge cases than as first-class golden additions.
