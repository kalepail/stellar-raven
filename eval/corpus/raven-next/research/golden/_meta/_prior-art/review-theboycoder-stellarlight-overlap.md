## Summary

Strongest additive candidates:

- `sl-priorart-lending-scf-flagships` - fills a real cross-protocol "lending is not greenfield + which flagships are funded" gap.
- `sl-hackathon-blend-winners` - useful as a second single-hackathon detail-expansion fixture if grounded.
- `sl-ecosystem-highest-scf-funded-ratio-category` - adds ratio-vs-absolute aggregate reasoning over ecosystem analytics.
- `sl-research-soroban-auth-recursion-dos` - adds a precise audit-record nuance not captured by broad Soroban security questions.

Likely duplicates:

- Oracles/Reflector, RWA issuers vs SCF grants, x402, SEP-41, regional builder search, broad crowded-vs-underbuilt category mapping, and passkey/smart-wallet repo discovery are already covered by current Raven questions.

Looks like cf-flue adapter tests more than Raven evidence-server questions:

- Exact `repoScore` leaderboards (`sl-code-passkey-wallet-top-repo`, parts of `sl-code-zk-xray-games-top`, `sl-code-soroban-oracle-least-bad`).
- Exact hackathon comparison counts (`sl-hackathon-kale-vs-blend-counts`), especially while Raven marks `scout_hackathon_compare` dormant.
- Fixed q2-2026 open-RFP title lists (`sl-funding-open-rfps-q2-2026`, `sl-funding-smart-account-passkey-rfps`), which Raven already treats as rolling freshness data.
- Coarse category-count claims that depend on a specific adapter taxonomy (`sl-ecosystem-asset-rwa-underbuilt-unfunded`).
